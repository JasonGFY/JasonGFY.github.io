Performance Management

Equity


The unfairness in performance evaluations can lead to dissatisfaction among team memebers. Most unfairness occures in the qualitative evaluation. So, how can we optimize the fairness? Here are 5 tips:
1. Make the evaluation content transparent
2. Make the evaluation standards transparent
3.Increase the frequency of evaluations
4.Reduce the differences in attention paid to subordinates
5.Collect information form multiple channels.


The guiding principle for performance assessment of agile teams is that in order to build high-performing agile teams. compared with some common principles of performance assessment, it places greater emphasis on motivation rather than assessment; on feedback and adjustment rather than mechanical execution. because innovation activities are highly uncertain, and the productivity of knowledge-based talent is more dependant on internal driving forces rather than external stimuli.


KPI(key performance indicators) and BSC(balance sheet card) are widely used in traditional organizations. OKR(objectives and key results) and 360-degress evaluation are very popular in agile organizations.
