Performance Management

Introduction


No matter which performance management tool is used, for an organization, there are only two goals: one is assist the organization in achieving its objectives, and the other is to motivate the employess.

Here, i would like categorize organizational form into two types, one is traditional organization, whice is categorized by having the clear goals and mature processes. the other is the agile organization which is defined by the fact that in vague and complex enviornment, all they know is the strategic goal. the type of performance management approach adopted depends on the type of organization. Agile organizations need to make frequent deliveries to obtain feedback, and adjust their plans promptly based on the feedback, team collaboration is the most important motivating factor. This is quite different from the traditional method of setting clear goals, formulating plans, and monitoring progress, competation is the most important motivating factor.


KPI(key performance indicators) and BSC(balance sheet card) are widely used in traditional organizations. OKR(objectives and key results) and 360-degress evaluation are very popular in agile organizations.
