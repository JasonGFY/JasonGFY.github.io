Steps in the classic competency study design

  1. Define performance effectiveness criteria
  2. Identify a criterion sample
  3. Collect data
      4. 6 data collection sources and methods
    • BEI behavioral event interivews
    • Expert panels
    • Surveys
    • Computer-Based "expert" system
    • Job task/function analysis
    • Direct observation
  4. Analyze data and develop a competency model
  5. Validate the competency model
  6. Prepare applications of the competency model

Conducting BEI

  • Objective: the interviewer's job is to keep pushing for complete stories that describe the specific behaviors, thoughts, and actions the interviewee has shown in actual situations.
  • Prepare for BEI: who / where (private)/ How (tape-record)/ Interview outline
  • BEI steps
    1. step 1. Introduction and explanation; 5-10min
    2. steo 2. Job responsibilites; 10-15min
    3. step 3. Behavior events
    4. step 4. Characteristics needed to do the job
    5. step 5. Conclusion and summary
