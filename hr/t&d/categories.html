Training & Development

Categories

T&D is a dynamic process, which constantly provides the competent staffs to support the company development. Company has its vision and strategy, whose staffs are the main resources to implement and accomplish the target. Therefore, T&D is the crucial tools, which company needs to utilize, to faciliate staffs development, to build a statble and potential workforce.


Training categories

  • Orientation
  • Capability Training
  • Skill Training
  • Mandatory Training

Training Methods
  • On-the-job training(OJT)
  • Job Instruction Training(JIT)
  • Lectures
  • Programmed Learning
  • Apprenticeship Training

T&D is one of the solutions, but is not the prescription for all management problems. When you receive the training request, first you should have a detailed conversation with the applicant. record the relevant issues, and conduct on-site observation and investigation to determine whether it is a capability problem or other type of problems.
