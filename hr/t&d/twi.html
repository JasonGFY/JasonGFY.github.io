Training & Development

Introduction

搭建岗位技能学习体系T&D is a dynamic process, which constantly provides the competent staffs to support the company development. Company has its vision and strategy, whose staffs are the main resources to implement and accomplish the target. Therefore, T&D is the crucial tools, which company needs to utilize, to faciliate staffs development, to build a statble and potential workforce.


TWI consists of 4 parts:

  • JI Job Instruction
  • JM Job Method
  • JS Job Safety
  • JR Job Relation

T&D is one of the solutions, but is not the prescription for all management problems. When you receive the training request, first you should have a detailed conversation with the applicant. record the relevant issues, and conduct on-site observation and investigation to determine whether it is a capability problem or other type of problems.
