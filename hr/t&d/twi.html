TWI

Introduction

TWI is the abbr. of Training Within Industry. It includes 4 j programs and problem solving. Job instruction (JI) training,instruct employees quickly remember how to do a job correctly, safely, and conscientiously. Job methods (JM) training, improve job methods in order to produce gereater quantities of quality products in less time by making the best use of the manpower, machines, and materials. Job relastions (JR) trainig, lead people, provide analytical method to effectively resolve problems that do arise.


TWI consists of 4 parts:

  • JI Job Instruction
  • JM Job Method
  • JS Job Safety
  • JR Job Relation

T&D is one of the solutions, but is not the prescription for all management problems. When you receive the training request, first you should have a detailed conversation with the applicant. record the relevant issues, and conduct on-site observation and investigation to determine whether it is a capability problem or other type of problems.
